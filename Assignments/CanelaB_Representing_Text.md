<h1>Representing Text with Binary numbers</h1>

My short sentence is <b>"I love playing volleyball"</b>
<br>
The ASCII representation for it is: 73, 76, 79, 86, 69, 80, 76, 65, 89, 73, 78, 71, 86, 79, 76,76,69, 89, 66, 65, 76,76.<br>
